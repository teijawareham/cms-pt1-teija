HUMBER HAWKS WBB

"SEASON RECAP"

The 2023-2024 season began with great anticipation for the Humber Hawks, as they embarked on their quest for another championship title. Led by star players Teija Wareham, Kia Watt, Sarah Baptie, and Katherine Khorovets, the Hawks boasted a formidable roster brimming with talent and determination. Throughout the regular season, the Hawks soared to new heights, steamrolling their opponents with their electrifying style of play and unyielding resolve. Teija Wareham dazzled crowds with her precision shooting, Kia Watt dominated the paint with her commanding presence, Sarah Baptie showcased her playmaking prowess, and Katherine Khorovets anchored the defense with her tenacity and skill. As the playoffs arrived, the Hawks were poised to make a deep run and cement their status as champions. However, fate had other plans in store. In a dramatic quarterfinal showdown, the Hawks found themselves facing off against the eventual gold medalists, the Richmond Ravens, in a clash of titans. Despite a valiant effort and a heroic performance from their star players, including Teija Wareham's sharpshooting, Kia Watt's dominant inside presence, Sarah Baptie's court vision, and Katherine Khorovets' defensive prowess, the Hawks fell just short of victory in a hard-fought contest. The heartbreaking defeat left the Hawks and their loyal supporters reeling with disappointment, as they came to terms with the bitter reality of falling short in their quest for championship glory. Yet, amidst the sorrow and anguish, the Hawks found solace in the knowledge that they had left everything on the court and had fought with courage and determination until the final buzzer sounded. As they reflect on the season that was, the Humber College Hawks are determined to use their playoff defeat as motivation to fuel their drive for redemption in the seasons to come. With a talented roster brimming with potential and a burning desire to reclaim their rightful place among the elite of collegiate basketball, the Hawks are poised to rise from the ashes and soar to even greater heights in pursuit of championship glory.
